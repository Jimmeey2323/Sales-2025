Physique 57 India Sales Plan 2025

January: New Year, New You

Capitalize on New Year’s resolutions and fitness enthusiasm.

  • Resolution Starter Pack: 5-class trial pack at INR 3,999.
  • Annual Membership Push: 10% off annual memberships for sign-ups before January 15.

Strategies:

  • Host an open house week with free trial classes and workshops.
  • Launch a "Commit to Fit" campaign on social media with client success stories.

February: Post-Holiday Slump

Drive trial conversions and re-engagement.

  • Valentine’s Duo Deal: Buy one 10-class pack, get the second at 50% off.
  • Weekend Warrior: 3-class weekend-only pack at INR 2,499.

Strategies:

  • Organize themed couple’s workouts for Valentine’s Day.
  • Offer personalized follow-up with trial attendees to convert them to memberships.

March: Spring Refresh

Leverage the change of season to promote rejuvenation.

  • Spring Renewal: 15% off on 3-month memberships.
  • Referral Rewards: Refer a friend and earn 2 free classes upon their signup.

Strategies:

  • Introduce a "Spring into Fitness" challenge: Attend 12 classes in March to win merchandise.
  • Collaborate with nutritionists for fitness and diet workshops.

April: Pre-Summer Prep

Attract clients seeking fitness solutions before summer vacations.

  • Summer Slim-Down: 20% off 10-class packages purchased before April 15.
  • Trial Week: Unlimited classes for one week at INR 2,999.

Strategies:

  • Host a "Pre-Summer Bootcamp" event featuring intensive workouts.
  • Run targeted digital ads promoting summer body transformations.

May: Summer Slowdown

Maintain momentum during vacation season.

  • Staycation Special: 10-class pack at INR 8,499 (valid for 3 months).
  • Family Fitness Package: Parent-child classes at 15% off.

Strategies:

  • Launch a "Sweat & Win" challenge: Complete 10 classes in May to earn discounts on renewals.
  • Partner with local resorts to offer exclusive fitness classes as part of stay packages.

June: Mid-Year Motivation

Re-engage existing clients and attract new ones.

  • Half-Year Sale: 10% off all memberships.
  • Student Special: Discounted summer classes for college students.

Strategies:

  • Host a "Mid-Year Check-In" event with goal-setting workshops.
  • Offer discounted personal training sessions to upsell memberships.

July: Monsoon Wellness

Promote indoor fitness as a monsoon activity.

  • Rainy Day Deal: 12-class pack at INR 9,999.
  • Trial Class Bonus: Sign up for a trial and get a free follow-up class.

Strategies:

  • Introduce a "Wellness Wednesday" series with free workshops.
  • Run email campaigns highlighting indoor fitness benefits during monsoons.

August: Monsoon Momentum

Reignite interest during another slow month.

  • Monsoon Boost: 20% off Unlimited Memberships.
  • Bring-A-Buddy Promo: Both clients get 2 free classes on signup.

Strategies:

  • Organize a themed fitness week with creative classes like "Dance in the Rain."
  • Offer exclusive deals for corporate clients to drive group signups.

September: Festive Prep

Gear up clients for the festive season.

  • Festival Fit Challenge: Attend 15 classes to win festive hampers.
  • Diwali Advance Sale: 10% off annual memberships purchased in September.

Strategies:

  • Collaborate with wellness brands for co-branded festive offers.
  • Run pre-Diwali detox and fitness workshops.

October: Festive Fitness

Maintain momentum amidst festive indulgences.

  • Fit Before Festivities: 3-month membership at INR 25,000.
  • Gift of Fitness: Buy a gift card and get 1 free class for yourself.

Strategies:

  • Host a "Diwali Glow-Up" event with beauty and fitness tips.
  • Promote memberships as ideal festive gifts through digital campaigns.

November: Year-End Push

Drive pre-holiday conversions.

  • Holiday Countdown: 6-month membership at 15% off.
  • Black Friday Flash Sale: 10-class pack at INR 7,999 (valid for 48 hours).

Strategies:

  • Launch a "15 in 30" challenge: Complete 15 classes in November for special rewards.
  • Partner with high-end brands for year-end giveaways.

December: Festive Cheer

Capitalize on holiday spending and New Year’s resolutions.

  • Year-End Deal: 10% off all memberships purchased in December.
  • Unlimited Holidays Package: 1-month unlimited classes at INR 15,000.

Strategies:

  • Host holiday-themed classes and events (e.g., "12 Days of Fitmas").
  • Run targeted ads highlighting fitness goals for the upcoming year.
